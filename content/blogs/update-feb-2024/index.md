# Monthly recap of Azure updates 

An easy-to-scan roundup of our newest, most important highlights. First month of the year has passed, but we already see a lot of Azure `Public Preview` and `General Availability` features announced. 

## Top 5 you don't want to miss

1. New Istio add-on upgrade an BYOK/CMK enhancements for AKS that improve Security.
2. Preview of intoducing Prometheus recommended alert in Azure Monitor.
3. Preview of hardening your AKS Clusters even more by disabling SSH support.
4. Preview of distributed tracing v2 for durable Functions.
5. Extension update for the VSCode AKS extension.

Look at the comprehensive list below for more information.
